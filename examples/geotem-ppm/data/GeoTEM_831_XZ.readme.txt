These data are from a 1996 Open File BHP AEM survey from North West Queensland, Australia.

The Geotem system was used a half-sine Tx current waveform of width 4.108 msec.  There were 16 off-time and 4-ontime dB/dt receiver windows.  The data are in units of parts per million (ppm) of the maximum dB/dt recorded during a high altitude reference flight where the receiver bird was nominally 120 m behind and 45 m below the transmitter loop.

See included logistics report Report_823.pdf.

Data format is as follows:

Column	Field
=============
1	line
2	easting
3	northing
4	tx_height
5-8	X_on_time
9-24	X
25-28	Z_on_time
29-44	Z
